About Developer

Hi, my name is Emmanuel!

I'm an experienced software engineer with over three years of expertise in diverse technologies, specializing in web development, data science, and the Internet of Things (IoT). Proven ability to leverage cutting-edge tools and methodologies to deliver impactful solutions across multiple domains.

I design and develop software solutions that meet and exceed client expectations by thoroughly understanding their requirements, implementing best practices, software architectures and design patterns, writing clean, efficient code to deliver high-quality, maintainable software.

Educational Background.

I began my journey in the world of technology with an Associate's degree in Information Technology from North Aguascalientes Technological University in Rincón de Romos, Aguascalientes, Mexico. I completed the program in August 2023, gaining a solid foundation in programming, databases, and systems analysis.

After finishing my associate's degree, I moved on to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Software Engineering at the same university. During this time, I delved deeper into coding, system design, and modern software development, taking on projects that helped me apply my knowledge to real-world challenges.

Skills

Throughout my journey, I’ve developed a solid skill set, particularly in:

  • Development of RESTful services using frameworks like Node.js/Express and Python/Django.

  • Working with databases, including MySQL, SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.

  • Applying Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles to create scalable and efficient solutions.
